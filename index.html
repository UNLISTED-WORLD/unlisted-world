What are Unlisted Shares?

Unlisted shares belong to companies that are not traded on public stock exchanges. These shares often offer early-stage investors an opportunity to participate in fast-growing businesses before they list via IPO. They can deliver high returns, but also come with risks such as lower liquidity and limited public information.

  • Benefits: Early access, potential high returns, diversification.
  • Risks: Lower liquidity, regulatory limitations, counterparty risk.
