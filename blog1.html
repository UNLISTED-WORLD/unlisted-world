How Pre-IPO Investing Works

Pre-IPO investing gives buyers a chance to buy shares of a private company before it goes public. Investors usually purchase lots from existing shareholders, and successful exits can be lucrative if the company lists at a higher valuation.

Takeaway: Understand allocation sizes, lock-in terms and the company's growth story before participating.
