Risks in the Unlisted Market

Unlisted investments are less liquid, often have limited public information and may involve counterparty or regulatory risk. Investors should have a clear exit plan and verify counterparties.

Tip: Maintain diversification and only allocate capital you can keep locked for medium to long term.
