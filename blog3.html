Valuation Basics for Private Shares

Common methods include comparable company multiples, discounted cash flow (DCF) and recent transaction pricing. For private companies, recent funding rounds and investor interest are strong signals of valuation.

Remember: Valuation is part art, part science — check multiple metrics before deciding.
